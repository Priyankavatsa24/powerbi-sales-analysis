## Project Objective

To analyze e-commerce sales data and build an interactive dashboard that helps business stakeholders 
- Monitor key sales and profit KPIs at a glance
- Identify top-performing states and customers 
- Understand payment mode preferences
- Track profit trends across months and quarters
- Compare product category and sub-category performance

## 📁 Dataset Description

### Orders.csv
- Order ID
- Customer Name
- State
- Order Date
- Sales Amount
- Profit

### Details.csv
- Category
- Sub-Category
- Quantity
- Payment Mode
- Average Order Value (AOV)

## Key Insights
- Maharashtra is the highest revenue-generating state, followed by Madhya Pradesh and Uttar Pradesh
- COD (Cash on Delivery) is the most preferred payment method at 44%, followed by UPI at 21%
- Clothing dominates product sales at 63% of total quantity, while Electronics accounts for 21%
- Printers and Bookcases are the most profitable sub-categories
- Profit dips are observed mid-year (June–August), suggesting seasonal slowdown
- When filtered to Goa in Q1, clothing accounts for 100% of sales — indicating a region-specific product preference
- Harivansh is the top customer by purchase amount
